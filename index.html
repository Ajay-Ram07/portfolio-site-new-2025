My Journey – From Struggles to Success

Early Education & Passion for Learning

Since childhood, I was a curious learner, excelling in academics & sports (cricket, football, and chess). I passed my GCE O/L with great results and chose the Biological Science stream for A/Ls.

A/L Season – The Biggest Challenge

During 2021-2022, my family’s financial struggles hit hard. I faced mental stress at school and made the toughest decision—I dropped out of school in 2021.

First Job & Entering the Tech World

I worked as an accountant, but my passion for learning didn’t stop. That’s when I discovered YarlIT Institute and met Yoha Seelan sir, who offered me a free seat in the Java class.

Facing Setbacks – But Never Gave Up

Due to commitment issues, the Java class was discontinued. But this time, I refused to fall back to zero. I took a job as a cashier at Arpico SuperCenter - Wellawatta and started learning Full-Stack Development at Novitech IT.

Becoming a Full-Stack Developer

I worked day and night, building real projects, mastering MERN stack, JavaScript, Python, and C. I earned my Web Development certification from SoloLearn and Graphic Design certification from Alison Academy.

The Future – Never Stop Learning

I am now studying Data Analytics & AI at Novitech IT, proving that degrees don’t define success—hard work does. My journey is built on passion, sleepless nights, and self-learning. And this is just the beginning!
